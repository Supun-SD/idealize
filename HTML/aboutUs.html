  About Us  

What is this TechWise?

TechWise is a web application that designed to provide school students in Sri Lanka with additional skill and knowledge in the field of information technology(IT).The platform offers a range of features to create a comprehensive learning experience and foster supportive community

Why?

Most of the schools in Sri Lanka do not have comprehensive IT programs or resources, leading to limited exposure to the field among students. This makes it challenging to create awareness if the foundation is not already established.

How to solve?

By providing educational materials, resources, and information about IT careers, the website can bridge the skills gap by introducing school children to the IT field at an early age. It offers them opportunities to explore different aspects of technology, learn coding basics, and gain familiarity with essential IT concepts. This exposure can inspire and nurture their interest in the field, ultimately encouraging them to pursue IT-related education and careers.

Vision

  1. "Empowering young minds with the wonders of technology, creating a future where every child in Sri Lanka has the opportunity to explore and excel in the world of IT."
  2. "Building a nation of digital pioneers, where IT education becomes a gateway to boundless opportunities and innovation."
  3. "A vision to inspire the next generation of tech leaders, fostering a digitally empowered Sri Lanka through comprehensive IT education."

Mission

  1. "Our mission is to ignite a passion for technology among Sri Lankan school children by providing accessible and engaging IT resources and educational materials."
  2. "To bridge the skills gap and empower students with fundamental IT knowledge, nurturing their curiosity and creativity in the digital realm."
  3. "We are on a mission to break barriers and transform the landscape of IT education in Sri Lanka, making it accessible to every child, regardless of their background or school resources."
  4. "Striving to create a future-ready generation, our mission is to equip students with the essential IT skills and knowledge needed to thrive in a technology-driven world."
  5. "With unwavering dedication, we aim to cultivate a culture of tech exploration, enabling young minds to unlock their full potential and become change-makers in the IT industry."

Key Features

  • Home : This page offers an introduction to the field of information technology,explaining the concepts,career opportunities and the relevance of IT skills in today’s world.it acts as a guide for students exploring the IT field.
  • About Us: The “About Us” page provides information about the platform’s mission,vision and the development team.
  • Resources: This act as a repository of educational materials,such as e-books,tutorials,videos and articles.
  • Community: The community aspect of the web app allows students to engage with one another,share thoughts,ask questions and seek advice.
  • Contact Us: This page provides contact information and a form to reach out to the platform administrator for feedback,technical support etc.
  • Login and Signup: This options available to create accounts and enroll to the platform.
